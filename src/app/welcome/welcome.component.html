Welcome to the Movie Trivia Quiz

This quiz will have 10 timed questions.

General Rules:

  1. Correct Question answered will give you 50 points!
  2. Incorrect Question answered will take away 50 points!
  3. You will have 10 seconds to answer each Question.
  4. Faster you answer a Question the more points you get for each problem.
  5. But if you answer a problem wrong you will also lose more points.
  6. Ex: if you answer a problem right in 9 seconds you will gain 9x50 points.
  7. Ex: if you answer a problem wrong in 4 seconds you will lose 4x50 points.
  8. Try to get the high score in the Leader Board.
